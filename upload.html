Contribute

Add shelf photos to a bookstore. Our OCR will read the spines — you'll have a chance to correct anything before it's saved.

Step 01 · Sign in

Sign in to contribute

We use passwordless email sign-in. Enter your email, and you'll get a one-click link to sign in. No password to remember.

Step 02 · Choose a shop

Which bookstore are you contributing to?

Go to the map, click a used bookstore pin, and press "Contribute photos →" to come back here with that shop pre-selected.

Step 03 · Upload photos

Drop shelf photos here

Horizontal photos of shelves work best. You can upload multiple at once. JPG or PNG, up to 5 MB each.

License: Photos and titles you contribute will be published under CC-BY-SA 4.0. By uploading, you confirm you took the photo (or have the right to license it) and agree to this license.
Step 04 · Save your contribution

Review, then save

When everything looks right, save. Your photos and confirmed titles will appear on the shop's page within seconds.
